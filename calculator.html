Financial Freedom Calculator

Know Your Number

How much do you need to never worry about money again? This calculator uses the 4% Rule from the Trinity Study to find your Financial Freedom Number.

The 4% Rule:

If you withdraw 4% of your investment portfolio each year, history shows the money will last 30+ years. That means your Financial Freedom Number = Annual Expenses × 25.

But military retirees have a cheat code: pension + VA disability reduce (or eliminate) how much you need from investments.

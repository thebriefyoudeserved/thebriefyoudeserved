About the Book

A practical guide for U.S. military service members that combines personal memoir with actionable advice. The mission: help you maximize your career, education, and financial opportunities during your service to achieve financial freedom and a fulfilling life after you transition out.

Written from an active duty perspective with a 20-year retirement focus, but the principles apply whether you're doing 4 years or 30.

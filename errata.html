Errata & Corrections

Keeping the information accurate

Military benefits, rates, and programs change. When we find errors in the book or digital resources — or when you do — we fix them here. This page is updated regularly.

Report an Error

Takes about 2 minutes. No account required.

How to Report an Error

1
Click "Report an Error" above. This opens a short form — no login or account needed.
2
Tell us where the error is. Which page, chapter, or section? The more specific you are, the faster we can fix it.
3
Tell us what's wrong and what's right. What does it currently say? What should it say? If you have a source (a website, regulation, or official document), include a link.
4
Submit. We review every submission. If we can verify the correction, we'll update the resource and add it to this page. Your name won't be shared unless you ask us to credit you.

VA Claims Guide & Federal Benefits — VA Disability Rates
Corrected: 2026-03-29
Was: 2025 VA disability rates (e.g., 100% single = $3,831.30/mo)
Should be: 2026 rates with 2.8% COLA (e.g., 100% single = $3,938.58/mo). All rates updated.
Scholarships Page — American Legion Legacy Scholarship
Corrected: 2026-03-29
Was: "Parent must be a member in good standing of The American Legion."
Should be: No Legion membership required. Eligibility is based solely on the parent's military service status.
Scholarships Page — DAV Membership
Corrected: 2026-03-29
Was: "DAV membership is FREE for disabled veterans."
Should be: DAV membership is a one-time $300 life membership ($10/mo payment plan available). Free for veterans age 80+. DAV claims assistance is free regardless of membership.
Scholarships Page — VFW Voice of Democracy
Corrected: 2026-03-29
Was: "$30K top prize"
Should be: $35,000 top prize (T.C. Selman Memorial Scholarship)
